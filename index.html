Apps

This site is the landing page for a suit of open source apps that improves your understanding and implementation of Service Levels as a tool to measure and improve reliability.

Calculator

The calculator takes the guesswork out of setting reasonable SLOs. It also helps you save your work in JSON for offline analysis or load it for tweaking in a slick UI.

  • Convert metrics to SLIs
  • Fine tune SLOs
  • See the alerting rules tied to those SLOs

Uptime Visualizer

This is a more advanced version of what you get from other tools with visualizations and flexibility to tweak your timeslices.

  • Model uptime scenarios in real-time
  • Interactively model different reliability scenarios with our dynamic uptime calculator.
  • Make informed decisions about appropriate availability targets for your specific service needs.
