SLI

SLIs can either be time-based or event-based.
The unit of the event that is measured by SLI. This is mainly used in the UI to make it easier to understand. Examples: requests, page views, logins, time window, etc.

Formula

Since the SLI is time based, the formula measures the percentage of good time divided by the valid time.

Since the SLI is event based, the formula measures the percentage of good events divided by the valid events.

SLI = time_length({{ sli.good }}) / time_length(total_time) * 100

Percentage of good events:

SLI = sum({{ sli.good }}) / sum({{ sli.valid }}) * 100
